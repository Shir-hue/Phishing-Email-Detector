Safer habits while reviewing suspicious emails:

Key reminders

  • Machine learning predictions may occasionally be incorrect. Always review suspicious emails carefully.

  • Be cautious of urgent requests, unexpected attachments, or links asking for credentials.
  • If using a workplace or university email account, pay attention to external email warnings provided by your organisation.
